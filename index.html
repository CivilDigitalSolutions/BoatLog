What BoatLog does

Everything your club needs, in one place

Built around the day-to-day of a rowing club, so committee members and rowers spend less time on admin and more time on the water.

Membership management

Keep member records, roles and renewals up to date. Know who's active, who's a coach, and who still needs to pay their subs.

Boat bookings

Reserve boats and slots without the double-bookings. See what's free, book in seconds, and keep the boathouse running smoothly.

Damage reporting

Log damage the moment it happens. Track repairs, flag boats as out of service, and keep everyone safe on the water.

How it works

Three steps to a well-run boathouse

1

Sign in

Members log in and book the boat and slot they want for their outing.

2

Row safe

Every outing is recorded. Any damage or issue is reported in a couple of taps.

3

Log out

Boats are checked back in, so the committee always has a clear, accurate picture.
